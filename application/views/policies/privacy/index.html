1. Business Description:
MOYATA E-commerce, is an E-commerce platform, means internet commerce where the
retail and Auctioneer of goods or services meet via the internet, and the transfer of money and
data to complete the sales. MOYATA E-commerce is a product of MOYA Technology, a
liability company incorporated in Rwanda under registration number . MOYA
Technology is registered with Rwanda Development Board (RDB), and MOYATA E-commerce
is also licensed with MINICOM.
Our main goal is to ease business activities by connecting the wholesaler and the
retailer. To attain our objectives, we introduced an E-commerce using Internet
through web application for business transactions and/or commercial transactions,
which typically involve the exchange of value across organizational or individual
boundaries in return for products and services between wholesaler and the retail.
